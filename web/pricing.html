How it works

GitSnip uses the GitHub API to fetch files. Your experience depends on how you authenticate:

Without a token

  • Shared limit: 60 requests/hour
  • May fail or slow down during peak
  • Good for quick, small downloads

With a token

  • Your own limit: 5,000 requests/hour
  • Much more reliable
  • Free to set up

Questions?

Why do I need a GitHub Token?
To avoid shared rate limits and unlock your own API quota. It's free and takes 30 seconds to set up.
Is my token safe?
Yes. Stored locally in your browser. Never sent to our servers.
Why is download speed limited?
To keep the service stable for everyone. Add a token or upgrade for faster downloads.
Can I cancel anytime?
Yes. Cancel with one click, no questions asked. You keep access until the end of your billing period.
What payment methods do you accept?
We use Stripe for payments. All major credit cards, Apple Pay, and Google Pay are supported.
